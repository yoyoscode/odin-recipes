Trader Joes Pizza

A quick, healthy homemade pizza that tastes better than takeout!

  • Trader joes premade pizza dough
  • TJ's pizza sauce
  • shredded mozzarella
  • pepperoni
  • jalepenos

Steps

  1. Set dough out of fridge for 20 minutes
  2. Flour surface. Take dough out and spread flour over it and lightly strecth it into a small circle.
  3. Using a rolling pin, roll the dough out into a 10-12 inch circle
  4. Lightly oil a pan and put pizza dough. Fix the dough as needed.
  5. Spread the as much sauce as you want, then cheese, pepperoni and finsh with jalepenos
  6. Bake for about 20 minutes or until golden on the edges.
  7. Let cool and enjoy!
